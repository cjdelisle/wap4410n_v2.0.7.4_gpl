Basic Wireless Settings


Wireless Network Mode You may choose B/G/N-Mixed if you have 802.11b ,802.11g and 802.11n on your network. If you would like to restrict your network to only 802.11b , 802.11g or 802.11n clients, then choose B-Only or G-Only mode. Choose Disabled if you would like to disable wireless access.
 
SSID The SSID is the unique name shared among all PCs in your network. The SSID must be the same for all the PCs on the network in order for them to communicate with each other. You may specify up to 4 SSIDs. (1 Main SSID and 3 Multiple SSIDs)
 
SSID Broadcast SSID broadcast enables the access point to identify itself as an available network to network scans. If you do not wish to announce the SSID to other devices, select Disable .
 
Wireless Channel The channel determines which frequency your network will operate in. If you are experiencing poor performance, try changing to another channel.
